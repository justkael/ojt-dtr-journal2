# Export Feature Guide

## What is the Export Feature?

The Export feature allows you to download copies of your **DTR logs** and **Weekly Reports** so you can save, print, or share them.

---

## Exporting DTR Logs

You can export DTR logs by selecting one or more entries from the table.

### Available file formats:
- **CSV** (can be opened in Excel or similar programs)
- **XLSX** (Microsoft Excel file)

Once exported, the file will automatically download to your device.

---

## Exporting Weekly Reports

Weekly Reports can be exported in two ways:

- **Single Export** – Open a report and export it individually.
- **Multi-Export** – Select multiple reports from the table and export them together.

### Important Reminder

Only **certified reports** can be exported.

If you select reports that are not certified:
- They will be automatically skipped.
- Only certified reports will be included in the download.

---

## Who Can Export?

### Admin
Admins can export:
- All DTR logs
- All Weekly Reports

### Intern
Interns can export:
- Only their own DTR logs
- Only their own Weekly Reports

---

## Notes

- Make sure a Weekly Report is **certified** before attempting to export.
- Exported files are saved directly to your device.
